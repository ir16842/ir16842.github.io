Products

The supplies we use:

  • Exterior

    1. Two buckets- one for soapy water and one for clean water
    2. Two non-scratch brush
    3. Car shampoo and a foam gun
    4. Microfiber rags- which will be used to dry the car, clean the windows, scrub the wheels and wipe off the wax or sealant
    5. Clay bar
    6. Wax or sealant
    7. Orbital buffer

  • Interior

    1. Vacuum
    2. ArmorAll Wipes- for cleaning and surface including the seats
    3. Glass cleaner and a microfiber rag
    4. Leather cleaner and conditioner- I use a product from Adams Polishes

  • Waxes & Sealants

    • H2O guard- usually used once the car has had a couple layers of wax or sealant applied, also used for monthly shine
    • Glass Wax- polymerizes in the sun- used for cars that rarely stay outside

  • Ceramic coating

    • Ceramic boost
